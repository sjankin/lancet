STATEMENT OF CONTINUED SUPPORT BY THE CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER (CEO) PERIOD COVERED BY OUR COMMUNICATION ON PROGRESS: JUNE 2015 TO JUNE 2016 Bombardier Inc. has been a participant of the United Nations Global Compact since 2007. I am proud to affirm my continued support of the company’s commitment towards the Compact and its principles. Since our last Communication on Progress in June 2015, we published our 2015 Activity Report to communicate our continued commitment to create financial and non-financial value across our business and to operate safely, ethically and efficiently.
