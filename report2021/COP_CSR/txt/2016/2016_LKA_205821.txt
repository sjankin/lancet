UNITED NATIONS GLOBAL COMPACT COMMUNICATION ON PROGRESS (COP) 2015 MABROC TEAS (PVT) LTD From: January 2015 To: December 2015 Mabroc Teas (Pvt)Ltd 2015/2016 1.0 Statement of Continued Support 14th January 2016 To Our Stakeholders, With all the difficulties experienced in the international market place during the year 2015, Mabroc Teas continued communicating the commitment to the principles of the United Nations Global Compact reaffirming the Ten Principles in the areas of Human Rights, Labour, Environment and Anti-Corruption. We are pleased to announce that Mabroc Teas is the first Sri Lankan Tea Company to obtain the Sri Lanka Tea Board Good Manufacturing Practices Certificate during the year under review. In addition to the Good Manufacturing Practices and Quality Assurance the scope of the certification also covers the hygienic and sanitary facilities provided to the employees. We would like to take this opportunity to wish the United Nations Global Compact continued success in their endeavors in promoting these noble principles around the world. Yours Sincerely, MABROC TEAS (PVT) LTD.
