2015 Communication on Progress Statement of Continued Support by the Chairman of the Board I am pleased to confirm that InsulFab Plastics, Inc. will continue to support the Ten Principles of the United Nations Global Compact regarding human rights, labor, environment and anti-corruption. In this annual Communication on Progress, we describe our actions to continually improve the integration of the Global Compact and its principles into our business strategy, culture and daily operations. Frank J. Hanus, III December 18, 2015 Human Rights Principles Assessment, Policy and Goals: InsulFab Plastics, Inc. is committed to ensure that all of our employees, job applicants, and suppliers are treated equally, respectfully, and without discrimination. Implementation: InsulFab has policies in place such as Equal Opportunity and Harassment Policy. Measurement of Outcomes: InsulFab’s suppliers haven’t received any violations or incidents regarding human rights violations in 2015. Labor Principles Assessment, Policy and Goals: InsulFab is committed to eliminating all discrimination in respect of employment. Implementation: InsulFab has policies in place such as a Non-Discrimination Policy. Global Compact Communication on Progress 2015 InsulFab Plastics, Inc. Measurement of Outcomes: No violations or investigations regarding the labor principles have been reported in 2015. Environmental Principles Assessment, Policy and Goals: InsulFab has an environmental policy describing our commitment to protecting the environment. Our goal in 2015 has been to digitize our records instead of filing away paper. Implementation: InsulFab implemented a new program to decrease printing by saving files on the computer. Measurement of Outcomes: This new program is reducing the effect on the environment. InsulFab has caused no major impacts on the environment in 2015. Anti-Corruption Principles Assessment, Policy and Goals: InsulFab will not participate in any corruption, extortion, or bribery with its suppliers or customers. We do not condone dishonesty or unethical business. Implementation: The accounting team is responsible for reporting any corruption, extortion, or bribery to executive management at InsulFab. Measurement of Outcomes: No corruption, extortion, or bribery was reported during 2015. Global Compact Communication on Progress 2015 InsulFab Plastics, Inc.
