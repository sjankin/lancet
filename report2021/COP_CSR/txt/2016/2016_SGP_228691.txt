Implementation of the 10 Principles of the UN Global Compact Over 8,400 businesses from 162 countries have pledged to uphold the 10 principles relating to human rights, labor standards, environmental protection and anti-corruption efforts defined in the UN Global Compact. In 2000, Credit Suisse became one of the first companies to sign up to this leading international agreement on responsible business practices. We report annually on the progress we have made.
