KG STATEMENT OF THE CEO ON THE PROGRESS REPORT TO THE UN GLOBAL COMPACT “Because sustainability is an important element of our long-term strategy, we have set ourselves 14 targets and we annually review our progress towards attaining these on a global basis. We aim, for example, to achieve a 10% reduction in the amount of fresh water consumed across our locations by 2020, and to cut our carbon emissions by 20%. We are well on the way to achieving these goals and we will consistently continue to pursue them. With our family-oriented human resources policy and the development of environmental-friendly product series we are already setting standards.” Hartmut Jenner Chief Executive Officer and Chairman of the Management Board Alfred Kärcher GmbH & Co.
