Dunamis Foundation is a non profit organization dedicated to achieve community empowerment through Leadership Development Activities. Dunamis Foundation was founded in 2001 with the name of Dunamis Mitra Pertiwi by the people who are concerned with leadership development and organizational alignment to assist the less fortunate and access-disadvantaged people of Pasir Angin, a village in Ciawi, Bogor as well in other parts of Dunamis Indonesia. FOUNDATION The fundamental motivating force in all activities of Dunamis Foundation is the development of people so that they are able to manage themselves, be aware of who they are, be able to share with others, and able to do all things with the principle of sharing and togetherness. Dunamis Foundation assists persons on the basis of need, not creed, race, or nationality. Under the auspices of such, Dunamis Foundation current project is DESA KEPEMIMPINAN-which aims to empower the village inhabitants in the vicinity of Dunamis Training facility in Pasir Angin, Gadog, Ciawi – West Java, to exercise their human rights.
