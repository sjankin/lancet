Ban Ki-moon Secretary-General United Nations New York, NY 10017 USA Communiciation on Progress Dear Mr. Secretary-General, We would like to take this opportunity to reconfirm our support of the UN Global Compact and our commitment to the ten principles with respect to human rights, labour, environment and anticorruption. Our commitment implies a continuous evaluation of our efforts within the field of corporate social responsibility and is an integral part of the daily work within our company. In order to reach as many of our stakeholders as possible with our Communication on Progress, we have continued to submit our sustainability report as an integrated part of our Annual Report ( enclosed). The sustainability report is found on page 42-61 and our sustainability goals are found on page 11. The sustainability report is also available as a separate report ( enclosed) and on our website (www.fabege.se). We report in accordance with level B+, GRI version 3.0 and the GRIindex is found on page 60-61. We are pleased to share our sustainable highlights in 2014 with you: Highest level in GRESB (Global Real Estate Sustainability Benchmark) survey: Green Star. Green Leases accounted for 86 per cent of total space under newly signed leases. Decision to proceed with BREEAM In-use environmental certification of the existing property management portfolio. Environmental certification of all new builds and major redevelopments, which amounted to five properties in 2014. The Fabege share was approved for inclusion in Robur's ethical funds and Nordea's Star funds. We have continued our focus on more efficient purchasing processes in order to enhance the control of our suppliers in terms of sustainability. At year end suppliers of 7 6 per cent of the total purchasing volume had been sustainability examined. Pro-active environmental work and to clearly state our high standards within business ehtics are success factors for us and we will continue our efforts within this field during 2015.
