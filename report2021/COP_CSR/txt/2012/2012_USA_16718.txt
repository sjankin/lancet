Brief description of nature of business Walla Walla Logistics was established by four global logistics businesses: Wallis Shipping Limited, Rotra Forwarding BV, MTI Worldwide Logistics and The HOLSHIP Group. Walla Walla Logistics, LLC operates upon the principle that freight forwarding and transportation are trusted and fundamental extensions of the businesses we serve. Walla Walla Logistics, LLC offers global services in the area of air, ocean and domestic freight and serves as a licensed customs brokerage as well. Statement of support Walla Walla Logistics, LLC is committed to the principles and goals of the United Nations Global Compact advancement of the ten universal principles in the areas of human rights, labor, environment and anti-corruption.
