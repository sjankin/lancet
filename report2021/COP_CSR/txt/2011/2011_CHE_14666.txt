Basel , decembre 2012 Oliver Johner Secretary General United Nations New York, NY 10017 USA Communication on Progress COP 2012 Dear Mr. Oliver Johner I am pleased to confirm that Corporate Fashion International AG continues to support the ten principles of the Global Compact in respect to human rights, labour rights, the protection of the environment and anti-corruption. With this communication, we express our intent to support and advance those principles within our sphere of influence. We commit to making the Global Compact and its principles part of the strategy, culture and day-to-day operations of our company and undertake to make a clear statement of this commitment both to our employees, partners, clients and to the public by internal media.
