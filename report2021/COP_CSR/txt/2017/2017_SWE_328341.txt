Gbo Fastening Systems AB Global Compact COP Annual reporting 2016 Mikael Jonsson Lars Dahl Q & E Manager MD Introduction Gbo Fastening Systems AB has a long proud history to share of producing high quality fasteners for safer constructions and buildings in the Nordics area. This success has been guided by strong corporate values of fairness and caring for employees, the local community and the surrounding environment. Over the years our business has gradually changed to become more international but still our core values remain the same. Joining and supporting the UN Global Compact program is natural step to continue our mission but in a broader perspective. 2 Environment Our company been ISO 14001 certified since 2012 but that is just a milestone in a long development where caring for the environment and conserving resources always been central for more than 250 years of production, at the same location.
