BANK OF AMERICA CORPORATION 2019 United Nations Global Compact Report UNITED NATIONS GLOBAL COMPACT The United Nations Global Compact (UNGC) is a voluntary initiative based on CEO commitments to implement universal sustainability principles and to take steps to support UN goals. Bank of America has been a signatory member of the UNGC since 2016 and supports the initiative’s ten principles on human rights, labor, environment and anti-corruption.
