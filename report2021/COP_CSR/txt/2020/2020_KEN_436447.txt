A brief description of Kenya Bixa Labour processes or systems • The employees are members of Kenya Union of Commercial Food and Allied Workers (KUCFAW), while the company is a member of Federation of Kenya Employees (FKE). The company advocate the standard working hours for Kenya bixa employees to be 44 hours a week and lunch break of 1 hour except those working on shift programs. • The recruitment process is structured and has a documented policy which strives to maintain diversity through gender, skills, race, values, attributes, and characteristics while offering equal opportunity to all potential candidates. We extend the same quality of selection to our vendors and partners. • Kenya Bixa Ltd don’t recruit persons under the age of 18 years since it’s a violation of children rights and we extend it to our vendors and partners and at the same time advocate for the abolition of child labour.
