Child Labor – CSGI does not engage in or support the use of child labor; young workers may only work 8 hours per day and not to be assigned in night shift. 2. Discrimination – no discrimination in hiring, promotion, etc.; no interference in the exercise of personnel rights, no threatening, abusive, exploitative behavior; and no pregnancy or virginity test. 6. Disciplinary Practices No corporal punishment, mental, physical or verbal abuse. 7. Working Hours – CSGI complies with applicable laws and industry standards, normal work hour per week not exceeding 48 hours; provision of 1 day off after every six days of work; voluntary overtime and not exceeding 12 hours per week. 8.
