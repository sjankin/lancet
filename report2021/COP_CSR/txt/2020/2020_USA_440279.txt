We survey all our vendors yearly regarding quality, environmental consciousness, and social responsibility. Over 95% of our vendors comply with Samtec’s standards, and 100% of our new suppliers are screened using human rights criteria. All Samtec employees are trained and expected to comply with the limitations of the Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA) and similar laws of the countries where we operate. We require suppliers to provide information regarding their own suppliers and subcontractors to verify that the extended supply chain related to Samtec products operates in a manner consistent with Samtec’s expected code of conduct and commitment to its environmental and social principles. Samtec will not tolerate Human Trafficking and/or Slavery crimes within Samtec or the suppliers with which we do business. We comply with the California Transparency in Supply Chains Act and the UK Modern Slavery Act of 2015. Samtec has a regular audit schedule of its supplier base, which must allow Samtec and its agents (including third parties) to engage in assessment activities to confirm compliance with these standards. We reserve the right, when the standards are not met and/or corrected in a timely fashion, to cancel outstanding orders, suspend future orders or terminate our relationship with the supplier, as circumstances demand. We currently have zero suppliers who have undergone human rights screening, are a significant risk for incidents of child labor or are involved in human trafficking.
