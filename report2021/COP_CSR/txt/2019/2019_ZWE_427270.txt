Systems reviews are audits of the procedures, processes and systems undertaken in targeted institutions /organisations/groups with the objective of curbing, eradicating or plugging loopholes in corruption prone areas in order to improve accountability, transparency, efficiency, effectiveness and promote good corporate governance.
