Our commitment implies a continuous evaluation of our efforts within the field of corporate social responsibility and is an integral part of the daily work within our company. In order to reach as many of our stakeholders as possible with our Communication on Progress, we have continued to submit our sustainability report as an integrated part of our Annual Report (enclosed). This year's report is the eighth in which Fabege is applying integrated reporting with the Annual Report in accordance with GRL Our sustainability goals are found on page 1 O and the GRI-index on page 99-100. The Annual Report is also available on our website (www.fabege.se). We are pleased to share our sustainable highlights in 2018 with you: 82% of total floor space is now environmentally certified according to BREEAM, which means that 100% of the propertymanagement portfolio is certified. Green Leases accounted for 96 per cent of total space under newly signed leases in 2018. 60% of our financing is now green In February 2018 Fabege received an external credit rating from Moody's ofBaa3, stable outlook. Fabege has started the construction of Scandinavias first zero-energy hotel, in Arenastaden in Solna. Our ambition to proactievly work for improved employee health continued with a number of different activities to encourage a long-term healthy lifestyle. We are also proud to annonce our partnership with SEI, Stockholm Environment Institute. In order to get a better idea of where Fabege stands in relation to global development we have initiated a partnership with SEI to carry out a sustainability analysis of our entire business strategy.
