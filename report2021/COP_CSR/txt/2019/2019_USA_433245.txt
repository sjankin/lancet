October 4, 2019 2019 Communication on Progress Bunge’s 2019 Communication on Progress for the United Nations Global Compact takes the form of the corporate web pages on the company’s global website, www.bunge.com/sustainability. It is a GRI Standards sustainability report and meets the Core-level, covering the company’s policies, actions and achievements for the 2018 reporting year, though includes information as recently as June 2019.
