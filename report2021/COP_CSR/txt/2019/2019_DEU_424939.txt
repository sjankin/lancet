CWS-boco International GmbH Franz-Haniel-Platz 6-8 47119 Duisburg United Nations Global Compact New York, NY 10017 USA January 11, 2019 Communication on Progress To whom it may concern, It is my great pleasure to inform you that CWS-boco International GmbH continues to support the 10 Global Compact principles in the areas of human rights, labour, the environment and anti-corruption.
