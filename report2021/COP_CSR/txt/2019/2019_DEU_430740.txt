UN Global Compact Communication on Progress 2018 Corporate responsibility is an integral component of Haniel’s corporate strategy. That was confirmed by the Holding Company when it signed up to the UN Global Compact on 27 March 2014 and when it committed again in 2018 to implement the 10 principles. In the 2018 Communication on Progress, the Haniel Holding Company reports on management systems and measures to implement the 10 principles in the areas of human rights, labour standards, environmental protection and anti­corruption.
