Embodiment of UN-SDGs in one of our Projects UN-SDGs In September 2015 Heads of State and Government from across the world agreed to set the world on a path towards sustainable development through the adoption of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development, which includes 17 Sustainable Development Goals, or SDGs — all to be achieved by 2030. The goals provide a framework for shared action “for people, planet and prosperity,” to be implemented by “all countries and all stakeholders, acting in collaborative partnership.” QDC recognizes the significance of and ways to adopt SDGs. Furthermore, we understand the essence of engineering solutions to urban (buildings, infrastructure, and landscaping) and industrial development, which are being and will continue to be central to determining the quality of life for a rapidly growing human population and the health of the planet as a whole. During delivery of our services to our valued customers, we explore all the opportunities to embed sustainability in our solution and align them with one or more of the 17 UN-SDGs. QDC makes significant and direct contribution to many SDGs, essentially by supporting our customers to develop livable buildings and infrastructure that prioritize social, environmental and economic sustainability.
