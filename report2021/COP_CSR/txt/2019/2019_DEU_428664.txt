Two washing and degreasing booths, three painting booths, a paint mixing room and a modern 4t conveyor system have been installed. By introducing a new washing and degreasing method for parts, HF Belišće has completely eliminated the use of solvent-based agents. The qualitative preparation of the parts by means of a high-pressure cleaning and degreasing system enable the process acceleration by the application of a single-layer metal paint Direct-To-Metal-Lack (DTM). So far, HF Belišće first had to apply the primer and then the topcoat. Fast drying of DTM even at lower temperatures leads to reduction of energy consumption for painting and drying. The factory also uses high-pressure paint pumps that improve the painting quality and reduces paint consumption per unit area. All together result in a higher painting quality, lower energy consumption and lower release of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) into the environment.
