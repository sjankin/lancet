Andreas Müller President & CEO Georg Fischer AG Amsler-Laffon-Strasse 9 8201 Schaffhausen Switzerland United Nations T +41 52 631 11 11 info@georgfischer.com Global Company Office georgfischer.com Two United Nations Plaza New York, NY 10017 USA Schaffhausen, 9 October 2019 Statement of continued support I am pleased to confirm that Georg Fischer (GF) reaffirms its support of the Ten Principles of the United Nations Global Compact in the areas of Human Rights, Labour, Environment, and Anti-Corruption. As a sign of our commitment to working on developing solutions to the existing societal issues and our belief in the power of multi-stakeholder collaboration, GF continues its membership with the Global Compact on a Participant level and actively contributes to the relevant discussions within the Swiss chapter of the UNGC organisation. In this annual Communication on Progress, we describe – through our Sustainability Report 2018 – our continuous actions to integrate the principles of the Global Compact into our business strategy, culture, and daily operations. We are committed to share this information with our stakeholders using our primary channels of communication. Yours sincerely Andreas Müller CEO Georg Fischer AG
