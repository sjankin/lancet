Microsoft’s Commitment to the UN Global Compact Microsoft endorsed the UN Global Compact in 2006. A decade later we remain firmly committed to the ten principles underlying the UNGC. Each year we communicate the progress we’ve made meeting the UNGC principles and Microsoft’s overall commitments to corporate social responsibility in an annual report. This statement serves as an addendum to our 2017 Microsoft CSR Report to express our ongoing commitment to the UNGC’s 10 principles.
