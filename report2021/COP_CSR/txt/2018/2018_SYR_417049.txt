Page_31 MURABAHA CAPITAL.LTD Principle 4: Elimination of all forms of forced labor: Murabaha has an administrative system that defines the relationship between it and its team members. Both parties sign a contract that identifies items of agreement including: type of work, office-hours, workdays, and vacations according to the members’ own circumstances. Murabaha gives fair salaries to its team members to grant them the minimum level to their living. It asks them if their salaries are not adequate. It provides them with necessary facilities (work-hours, leaving permissions, vacations…).
