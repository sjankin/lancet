The award is given to a company which has the best annual environmental or sustainability report prepared. We hope that the Green Frog Award will become a symbol of promoting environmental culture among companies in the region. According to Deloitte, annual financial statements of the companies in the region would not be solely limited to the financial implications of the companies' business, although companies prepare environmental and sustainability reports on voluntary basis.
