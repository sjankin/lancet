Brambles Limited ABN 89 118 896 021 UN Global Compact Communication on Progress 2013 18 October 2013 Period covered From: 1 July 2012 To: 30 June 2013 Statement of support by the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) To our stakeholders: I am pleased to confirm Brambles’ support of the Ten Principles of the United Nations Global Compact in the areas of Human Rights, Labour, Environment and Anti-Corruption. Brambles became a signatory to the United Nations Global Compact in June 2013. In this annual Communication on Progress, we describe our actions to integrate the Global Compact and its principles into our business strategy, culture and daily operations. We also commit to share this information with our stakeholders through our website and annual reporting.
