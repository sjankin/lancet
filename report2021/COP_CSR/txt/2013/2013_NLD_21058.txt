Aalten, 26.2.2013 To our stakeholders: I am pleased to confirm that Dibella reaffirms its support of the Ten Principles of the United Nations Global Compace in the areas of human rights, labour, environment and anti-corruption. In this attached annual Communication on Progress we describe our actions to continually improve the intergration of the Global Comapct and its principles into our business strategy, culture and daily operations.
