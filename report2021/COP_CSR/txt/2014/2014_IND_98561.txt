Rashtriya Ispat Nigam Limited Visakhapatnam Steel Plant Visakhapatnam Dear Stakeholders, It is eight years since RINL/VSP has joined the United Nations Global Compact. RINL showed great resolve and commitment in carrying forward its initiatives under Corporate Social Responsibility even in adverse global steel market scenario. RINL ever remained committed to all the principles of Global Compact in the areas of Human Rights, Labour, Environment and Anti-corruption. RINL/VSP incorporated the major principles of Global Compact in its various Corporate policies and a number of projects could be taken up to implement the same. Efforts shall continue towards Corporate Governance and to contribute to society as a good Corporate Citizen. We, the collective of RINL, reaffirm our commitment to UNGC Principles and efforts to achieve safe, healthy & vibrant surroundings.
