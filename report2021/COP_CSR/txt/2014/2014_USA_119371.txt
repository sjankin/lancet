2014 Communication on Progress Statement of Continued Support by the Chairman of the Board InsulFab Plastics, Inc. will continue to support the Global Compact Principles regarding human rights, labor, environment and anti-corruption. We continue to integrate these principles into our business operations every day. In 2014, our Communication on Progress describes our initiatives for implementation of the principles and the outcomes for each of the principles. Frank J. Hanus, III December 18, 2014 Human Rights Principles Assessment, Policy and Goals: InsulFab Plastics, Inc. is committed to supporting the protection of internationally proclaimed human rights. Implementation: InsulFab communicates with customers that every supplier used supports the protection of human rights. Measurement of Outcomes: InsulFab’s suppliers haven’t received any violations or incidents regarding human rights violations in 2014. Labor Principles Assessment, Policy and Goals: InsulFab upholds the freedom of association and recognizes the right to collective bargaining. Global Compact Communication on Progress 2014 InsulFab Plastics, Inc. Implementation: The employee rights notice recognizing collective bargaining is posted in an area available to all employees. Measurement of Outcomes: No violations or investigations regarding the labor principles have been reported in 2014. Environmental Principles Assessment, Policy and Goals: InsulFab has an environmental policy describing our commitment to protecting the environment. Our goal in 2014 has been to recycle more scrap metal. Implementation: InsulFab implemented a new scrap program to increase the recycling of scrap metal that results from production. Measurement of Outcomes: This new program is reducing the effect on the environment. InsulFab has caused no major impacts on the environment in 2014. Anti-Corruption Principles Assessment, Policy and Goals: InsulFab will not participate in any corruption, extortion, or bribery with its suppliers or customers. Implementation: The accounting team is responsible for reporting any corruption, extortion, or bribery to executive management at InsulFab. Measurement of Outcomes: No corruption, extortion, or bribery was reported during 2014. Global Compact Communication on Progress 2014 InsulFab Plastics, Inc.
