Conrico International Communication on Progress Period Covered: February 13th 2014February 13th 2015 H.E Ban Ki-moon Secretary-General United Nations New York, NY 10017 USA Dear Mr Secretary-General, I am pleased to confirm that Conrico International Limited reaffirms its support of the Ten Principles of the United Nations Global Compact in the areas of human rights, labour, environment and anticorruption.
