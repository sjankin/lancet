Triacta Power Technologies Communication on Progress to The Global Compact for the Calendar Year 2013 Please find enclosed our Communication of Progress for calendar year 2013. We have summarized our activities pursuant to the Global Compact, outlining our achievements up to the end of December. We have included an update to our Report Card providing a quantitative summary of the progression of corporate policies, procedures and employee education against each of the Global Compact’s 10 principles of corporate behaviour. Triacta Power remains supportive of the Global Compact and committed to development of a corporate culture that through basic policy implementation and education operates under the 10 basic principles. Wes Biggs P. Eng. President & CEO Triacta Power Technologies Inc. Activities and Completion Success through 2013 with Relevance to the Global Compact As a small company with limited resources Triacta’s focus with respect to the Global Compact remains on area’s of activity that are aligned to one or more of the Global Compact’s 10 Principles and bring near-­‐term operational and corporate culture benefit to the company. A status summary of the activities undertaken in 2013 pursuant to the Global Compact and completion level against those activities is included in the report along with a historical Report Card which scores our annual progress in advancing the 10 principles of the Global Compact within Triacta. Activities for the Calendar Year 2013 Activity 1 – Policy & Procedure Review and Update Establish or Augment written policies and procedures relevant to the Environment & Anti-­‐Corruption. Status -­‐ in process. Policies have been reviewed, updates are in draft and will be incorporated in employee handbook in 2014. Activity 2 – Employee Awareness/Participation Program i) Create a condensed version of the Employee Handbook(pamphlet and/or web version) to provide a quick reference guide on Human Rights, Labour, Environmental and Ethics Policies at Triacta. Status – in process. Target completion in 2014. ii) Establish information sessions for the employees to educate and inform with respect to company policies and best practices. Status – complete, information sessions are now implemented as part of the company’s regular safety information sessions. Updated Performance Metrics for the Calendar Year 2013 Included is a report card on the company’s initiatives and completeness in implementing policies and procedures that embody the 10 principles of the Global Compact. The company is scoring itself from 0-­‐10 on its operational procedures and policies for each principle. It will not be the intention of the company to target improvements each year in each category but instead focus on specific areas that provide both near or mid-­‐term operational and/or corporate culture benefit and align with Global Compact Principles.
